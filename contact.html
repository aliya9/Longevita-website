Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How does Longevita personalize my wellness plan?

Longevita uses AI to analyze your daily symptom check-ins, preferences, and health goals to create a personalized plan. The more you use the app, the better it understands your unique needs.

Is my health data secure?

Absolutely! We use enterprise-grade encryption and follow strict privacy guidelines. Your health data is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

Can I use Longevita with my existing health apps?

Yes! Longevita integrates with popular health apps and devices like Apple Health, Google Fit, Fitbit, and more to give you a comprehensive view of your wellness.

What if I don't like the recommendations?

You can always adjust your preferences and provide feedback. Our AI learns from your choices to provide better recommendations over time.
